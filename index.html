Computer Fundamentals and Programming

In my Computer Fundamentals and Programming subject, I learned about the history of computers and how technology has evolved over time. I explored the advantages and disadvantages of using computers in daily life, as well as how the internet works and its impact on communication and information sharing. I also learned how to write pseudocode to plan out simple programs before coding. Additionally, I was introduced to web development basics, including HTML for structuring web pages and CSS for styling them. This subject has given me a solid foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of computing.
